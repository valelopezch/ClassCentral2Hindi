The 100 Most Popular Free Online Courses (2023 Edition)

Using Class Central data and enrollments numbers to find the 100 most popular online courses of the year.

Suparn Patra 6 दिसंबर, 2022
  • फेसबुक
  • ट्विटर
  • लिफ़ाफ़ा
  • 0

More than a decade has passed since a few स्टैनफोर्ड professors decided to offer their courses online for free. These later became known as MOOCs, for Massive Open Online Courses, and 2012 became the “Year of the MOOC.”

Since then, the online course offering has continued to grow, to the point that it can be hard to navigate at times. In this article, I’ve leveraged Class Central‘s database of over 100,000 courses, as well as course enrollment numbers, to build a list of the 100 most-popular online courses of the year.

क्रियाविधि

मैंने इस सूची को एक अच्छी तरह से परिभाषित पद्धति के बाद बनाया है:

First, I went through the Class Central database and made a list of all the courses that were offered for the first time ever in 2022. This represented around 2600 courses.

Then, I wrote scrapers to collect enrollment numbers across four major online course platforms: Coursera, एडएक्स, फ्यूचरलर्न, and Swayam. These platforms show how many students are enrolled in each of their courses.

Finally, I sorted the courses according to their enrollment number and truncated the list to keep only the 100 most-popular courses.

Combined, the platforms considered accrued over 4M enrollments for courses launched in 2022. The 100 most-popular courses account for about half of them.

Interestingly, about 25% of the courses were created by Google, and about 15% by Meta and by आईबीएम. Tech companies are well-represented in this year’s list.
